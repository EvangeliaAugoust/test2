Creative, Passionate UX/UI Designer

Hello there! My name is Evangelia & I'm an aspiring UX/UI Designer currently based in Thessaloniki, Greece.

I taught myself UX/UI design because I love creating digital stuff that looks great and is easy to use. I've learned a lot by trying things out, experimenting with new ideas, and working on different projects. I'm all about making websites and apps that people enjoy using. I pay close attention to details and always try to come up with cool ideas.

As a Computer Science graduate, I enjoy bridging the gap between technology and design, combining my technical expertise with a strong sense of aesthetics to create beautiful, user-friendly products.

❛❛ My ultimate goal is to design applications that are not only efficient and scalable under the hood, but also deliver pixel-perfect user experiences that engage and delight. ❜❜

In my free time, you’ll find me exploring hidden spots in the city, capturing street photography, or getting lost in indie music while working on creative and inspiring crafts with clay and other materials. A fun fact about me: I’m a huge fan of cinematography and enjoy exploring the art behind filmmaking. ✨

Let's make something awesome together!
